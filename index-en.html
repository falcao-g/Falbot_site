Features

Gamble comands

Bet in the winning horse, pray that the slot machine is in your favor. Falbot has various ways to test your luck with high rewards.

User interaction

Falbot has different ways of user interaction, from horse race duels to even share some of your wealth, things become even more competitive with the global and local ranks!

Fun commands

Not all in life is about earning money and gambling, sometimes you just want to laugh with some silly commands, don't worry, Falbot has those too.

Utilities

Need to roll some dices or calculate a math equation? Falbot has useful commands that can help you on the daily routine

Commands list

A list with all commands can be found below, but you will always be able to acess these informations by using/commands when using the bot

OBS: Falbot's default prefix is ? but it can be changed at any moment using /prefix

Game commands

Name Description
/balance Shows your or another user's balance
/lootbox Redeem your free lootbox (available every 12 hours)
/donation Donate x Falcoins to the mentioned user
/horse Guess the winner horse
/rank Show the local or global rank
/store Show the available items in the shop
/roulette Bet in the roulette
/slot Bet in the slot machine
/bank Deposit or withdraw your falcoins from the bank
/fight Challenge a user to a fight until death
/crate Spend keys and boxes to have a chance to win some prizes
/horseduel Challenge a user to a horse race
/russianroulette Play with your friends, users can enter reacting to the message
/tictactoe Challenge a user to a tic-tac-toe game
/vote Redeem your reward for voting in the bot on top.gg
