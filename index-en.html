Features

Gamble comands

Bet in the winning horse, pray that the slot machine is in your favor or gamble in the roulette. Falbot has various ways to test your luck with high rewards.

User interaction

Falbot has multiple user interaction features, challenge your friends to games betting falcoins or even share a little of your money. Make friends and foes in your climb to the top of the leaderboard!

Ranks

Rank-up as you progress in the game, ranks give you rewards that help you earn even more falcoins. Ranks also have its own leaderboard.

Fun commands

Want to have fun without worrying about falcoins? Don't worry, Falbot has commands made just for that.

Utilities

Need to roll some dices or calculate a math equation? Falbot has useful commands that can help you on the daily routine

Language

All Falbot features are available in both portuguese and english!
