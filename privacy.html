Falbot Privacy Policy

Up to date as of 19/01/2023

We value your privacy and try to minimize the data we collect from you. This page is just here to let you know what information is stored so that the service can operate.

No third-party tracking tools or cookies are utilized on our website.

By creating an account or interacting with our applications, we collect information required for the service to operate. This is limited to information from Discord itself, mainly your Discord Id and username necessary for the application to function. All other pieces of information stored are generated when the user interacts with the Service (ex. Falcoins, rank).

Users may request that their account be terminated and personal data be deleted by emailing us at falbot.oficial@gmail.com. Requests for account termination will be processed as soon as they are received and user data will be permanently erased within 30 days of termination. Also, if you and Falbot doesn't share a server anymore, your data will be deleted automatically.
